GCC Entrepreneurs Forum 2024

Under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Hamad bin Mohammed Al Sharqi, Deputy Ruler of Al-Fujairah

An annual event dedicated to enhancing cooperation and innovation within the entrepreneurial ecosystem in the GCC countries.
A forum that brings together the leading minds and experts in the field of entrepreneurship in the GCC countries. This forum comes as a confirmation of the importance of innovation and collaboration in enhancing the business environment, serving as a meeting point for experts, decision makers, investors, and ambitious entrepreneurs. The forum is considered a unique opportunity to explore future trends, share best practices, and build a network of relationships that support growth and progress in the field of entrepreneurship. Through workshops, discussion sessions, and inspiring lectures, we aim to stimulate creativity and knowledge exchange among all participants. Let's work together to enhance the business environment in the GCC, support ambitious entrepreneurs, and build a bright and prosperous future for the region.

A special session for officials from the entities responsible for entrepreneurship in GCC countries and the GCC General Secretariat (open discussion) on the following topics:

  • The role of entrepreneurship in the Gulf economy
  • Enablers and incentives to support entrepreneurship
  • Foreseeing the future of entrepreneurship in the Gulf economy

  • Business accelerators in GCC countries
  • The role of entrepreneurship in enhancing the innovation and artificial intelligence ecosystem
  • Developments in marketing startups
