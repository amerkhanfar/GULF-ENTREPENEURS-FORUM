About the event

Under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Hamad bin Saif Al-Rashdi, Deputy Ruler of Al-Fujairah

The annual Gulf Entrepreneurs Forum aims to enhance the complementary role between institutions involved in the entrepreneurship sector and small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries. This is achieved by developing mechanisms for cooperation among participating parties to overcome the challenges faced by GCC countries in the growth and development of the sector, and by discussing investment opportunities for entrepreneurs and funding for SMEs.

  • Where

    Fujairah, United Arab Emirates

  • When

    21-22 November 2024
