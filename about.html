About The Conference

GULF ENTREPRENEURS FORUM 2024

Under the patronage of His Highness Sheikh Hamad bin Saif Al-Rashdi, Deputy Ruler of Al-Fujairah

Welcome to the Gulf Entrepreneurs Forum 2024, an annual event dedicated to fostering collaboration and innovation within the entrepreneurship ecosystem of the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries.

Objectives
  • Facilitate stronger connections between institutions and SMEs to support mutual growth and success.
  • Identify and tackle the obstacles hindering the advancement of the entrepreneurship sector in the GCC.
  • Discuss and uncover investment prospects and funding opportunities for entrepreneurs and SMEs.
KEY HIGHLIGHTS

Inspiring Keynote Speakers: Hear from leading experts and influential figures in the entrepreneurship and SME sectors.

Networking Opportunities: Connect with fellow entrepreneurs, investors, and industry professionals.

Panel Discussions: Engage in thought-provoking discussions on current trends and future prospects in the entrepreneurship landscape.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND

Entrepreneurs and business owners

Investors and venture capitalists

Policy makers and institutional representatives

Industry experts and consultants

ABOUT THE FORUM

The Gulf Entrepreneurs Forum 2024 aims to strengthen the partnership between institutions and small and medium enterprises (SMEs) throughout the GCC. Our mission is to develop effective mechanisms for cooperation among various stakeholders, address the challenges faced by entrepreneurs and SMEs, and explore new investment opportunities.
